Getting Here from El Paso

It's easier than you think — here's how

1

Cross the Border

Use the Stanton Bridge or Bridge of the Americas. Crossing typically takes 5–15 minutes by car.

2

Drive to Plaza Sendero

Head to Av. Francisco Villarreal Torres #2050. It's a major shopping plaza — easy to find with GPS. About 10 min from the bridge.

3

Find the Medical Center

Go to Centro Médico de Especialidades inside Plaza Sendero. Free parking available at the plaza.

4

Walk In or Check In

No appointment needed! Walk in between 3:00–5:00 PM, Mon–Fri. Or message us on WhatsApp to let us know you're coming.

🛂 What You Need to Cross Back to the U.S.

Make sure you have your valid passport or passport card (or SENTRI/Global Entry if applicable). U.S. citizens and permanent residents can cross freely. The return crossing to El Paso usually takes 15–40 minutes depending on the time of day.
