Getting here from El Paso

It's easier than you think — here's how

1

Cross the Border

Use the Stanton Bridge or Bridge of the Americas. Typically 5–15 minutes by car.

2

Drive to Plaza Sendero

Head to Av. Francisco Villarreal Torres #2050. Easy to find with GPS. ~10 min from bridge.

3

Find the Clinic

Go to Centro Médico de Especialidades inside Plaza Sendero. Free parking available.

4

Walk In

No appointment needed! Walk in between 3:00–5:00 PM, Mon–Fri.

🛂 What you need to cross back: Make sure you have your valid passport or passport card (or SENTRI/Global Entry). U.S. citizens and permanent residents can cross freely. Return crossing takes 15–40 minutes.
